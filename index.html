Frequently Asked Queries

Why Fanoon?

Fanoon is dedicated to showcasing the rich and diverse art and talent of Pakistan. Our platform is free to use, providing artists with a space to display their work and reach a wider audience. We believe in the power of art to connect people and celebrate cultural heritage.

Are my payments secure?

Yes, all payments on Fanoon are secure. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure that your transactions are safe and protected. Your trust is important to us, and we strive to provide a secure environment for all users.

How to register an account?

Registering an account on Fanoon is simple and free. Just click on the 'Register' button at the top right of the homepage, fill in your details, and start showcasing your art. As a member, you can create a profile, upload your artwork, and connect with other artists and buyers.

Does Fanoon deliver outside Pakistan?

Yes, Fanoon supports international delivery. Our goal is to bring Pakistani art to the global stage, and we work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your artwork reaches its destination safely, no matter where you are in the world.

Is Fanoon free?

Yes, Fanoon is completely free to use. Our mission is to provide a platform for artists to showcase their talent without any financial barriers. We believe in supporting the arts and making it accessible for everyone to appreciate and purchase beautiful artwork.
